The files in this repository are part of the final project for ENGL 502, Intro to Technical and Professional Communication, completed at the University of New Mexico Department of English in Fall 2025.

The course involved working through the steps of evaluating the usability of a chosen artifact. The artifact tested for this project is the Student Forms page from the Office of the Registrar at UNM: https://registrar.unm.edu/forms/index.html
